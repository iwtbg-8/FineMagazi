At present, FineMagazi does not collect personal data directly from users, namely names, e-mail addresses, or payment details.

Data collected by these third-parties will be governed by their respective privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights:

Considering that FineMagazi does not collect personal data directly, these rights would mostly apply to third-party tools (for instance, Google).

6. Children's Privacy
